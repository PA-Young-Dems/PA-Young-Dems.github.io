Criminal Justice Reform

Mass Incarceration

Nearly 400,000 people in Pennsylvania are a part of the Criminal Justice System. Over 175,000 are on parole. And over 100,000 Pennsylvanians are incarcerated. Currently, Pennsylania is among the highest around the country for people incarcerted per 100,000 at 640. Tens of thousands of prisoners are incarcerated before thay even have a trial. The racial disparity of incarceration in Pennsylvania is nearly 9:1, with African Americans incarcerated at a rate of 3,300 per 100,000 compared to 375 for Whites.

Drug Policy

The War on Drugs is a failure. We need a sensible drug policy in Pennsylvania. Arcane drug policies are a leading cause of racial disparity in sentencing and incarceration. Decriminalizing marijuana possession is a necessary step to ensure thousands of people are not unnecessarily locked up.
