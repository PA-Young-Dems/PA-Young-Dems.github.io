Mission statement

The Pennsylvania Young Democrats are the official youth wing of the Pennsylvania Democratic Party. It is our mission to encourage civic engagement in the party and empower young people across our commonwealth to elect Democrats up and down the ballot while organizing and advocating for progressive values. We are the future of the Pennsylvania Democratic Party, and the future is now.

Change will not come if we wait for some other person or some other time.
We are the ones we've been waiting for.
We are the change that we seek.
